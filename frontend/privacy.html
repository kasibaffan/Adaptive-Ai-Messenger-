1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: company name, email address, and password (stored as a salted hash, never in plain text) when you register.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information above to: operate and improve the Service; authenticate your account; power your assistant's responses to your customers; deliver the reminders you schedule; process payments and manage subscriptions; and communicate with you about your account.
